Malaysia's independence heralded a time when good education is imperative to the nation's development and economy due to the high demand for highly skilled local talents.
At this time, our institution, named after the Father of Independence YTM Tunku Abdul Rahman Putra Al-Haj, was founded as TAR College to answer the call to provide that education opportunity to all Malaysians, regardless of background, race or creed.
